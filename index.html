About Me

Hello! My name is Mohammed Shebli, and I'm 31 years old. I graduated from Al-Najah National University with a bachelor's in business administration. For the past eight years, I have worked as a banker.
I started as a teller at Cairo Amman Bank and worked my way up to become the head of the corporate credit department, gaining extensive experience in financial analysis and providing superior customer service.
After 8 years of having a successful career in the financial sector, I realized I was boarding the wrong plane and had to take the risk of jumping out.
For the past year, I've been studying and gathering the knowledge I need to land my first front-end developer position. I started out learning about object-oriented programming concepts and data structures, then moved on to HTML and CSS, then had a fair understanding of Javascript, and now my final stop is mastering ReactJS before I start applying for jobs.
